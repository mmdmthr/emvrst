# Special Fried Rice

Fried rice is one of Indonesia's most popular traditional dishes. This dish is easy to make and can be customized to individual tastes.

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare Spices**: Grind garlic, red chilies, and shrimp paste until smooth.

2. **Sauté Spices**: Heat oil in a wok, sauté the ground spices until fragrant.

3. **Add Eggs**: Crack eggs into the wok, stir until half-cooked.

4. **Add Rice**: Add the white rice, mix well with spices and eggs.

5. **Season**: Add sweet soy sauce and salt, stir until the rice changes color.

6. **Serve**: Fried rice is ready to serve while hot.

**Tips**: Use cold rice so it doesn't break easily when stirred.
