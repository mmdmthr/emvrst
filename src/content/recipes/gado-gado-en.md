# Betawi Gado-Gado

Gado-gado is an Indonesian salad consisting of boiled vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and eggs with delicious peanut sauce.

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare Vegetables**: Boil water spinach, bean sprouts, and potatoes until tender.

2. **Fry Accompaniments**: Fry tofu and tempeh until golden brown.

3. **Make Peanut Sauce**: 
   - Roast peanuts until cooked
   - Grind with ground spices
   - Add water, salt, and palm sugar to taste

4. **Serving**: Arrange all ingredients on a plate, pour with peanut sauce.

**Tips**: Peanut sauce can be made thinner or thicker according to taste.
